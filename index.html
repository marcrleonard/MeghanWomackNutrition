Nutrition for Adults

Providing nutrition guidance on healthy living, specific diagnoses, eating disorders, sports nutrition, food allergies and intolerances, gastrointestinal issues, and weight concerns. Always client centered and based on science, not fads and trends.

Nutrition for Kids and Families

When a child’s nutrition goes awry, it can often affect the entire family. Together we will collaboratively address growth concerns, picky eating, food allergies, negative body talk, nutrition for sports, and even eating disorders.

Community Outreach

Education is our society’s most powerful tool for prevention. I love teaching people, adults and kids alike, about various aspects of health and nutrition in order for them to be able to enjoy food as an essential piece of health and happiness.
