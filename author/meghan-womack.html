About

I been practicing as a registered dietitian for over 10 years. I have a Masters degree in Nutrition Sciences from The University of New Mexico and I am a certified eating disorders registered dietitian. I am passionate about helping my clients to find their own unique rhythm of eating that serves their health and quality of life. I practice from a Health at Every Size perspective and believe each person can rediscover an intuitive relationship with food and movement.
